# Product Overview

AI 학생 비서 (AI Student Assistant) — a Korean-language personal dashboard for university students. It provides schedule management, academic tracking, administrative info, and an AI chat interface.

## Core Sections
- Landing page with daily briefing slider and AI chat bar
- Dashboard with profile summary and navigation cards for three domains
- Schedule: calendar, upcoming events, and a to-do checklist
- Academic: grades, exam prep, and in-progress assignments
- Admin: extracurricular activities, contests, and communication history

## Target User
Korean university students (UI text and data are entirely in Korean).
